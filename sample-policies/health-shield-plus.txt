Health Shield Plus - Comprehensive Health Insurance Policy
Insurer: ABC Insurance Company Ltd.

POLICY OVERVIEW
Health Shield Plus is a comprehensive health insurance plan designed for individuals and families seeking extensive coverage with affordable premiums. This policy provides financial protection against medical expenses arising from hospitalization, surgeries, and critical illnesses.

PREMIUM AND COVERAGE
Annual Premium: ₹15,000 (for individual aged 30-40)
Sum Insured: ₹5 Lakhs
Policy Term: 1 year (renewable)
Family Floater Option: Available with additional premium

WAITING PERIODS
Initial Waiting Period: 30 days from policy inception for general illnesses
Pre-existing Conditions: 24 months waiting period
Specific Diseases: 12 months for conditions like hernia, cataract, joint replacement

INCLUSIONS - WHAT'S COVERED
1. Hospitalization Expenses: Room rent, nursing charges, doctor's fees, medicines, diagnostic tests
2. Pre-hospitalization: 60 days before admission
3. Post-hospitalization: 90 days after discharge
4. Daycare Procedures: Treatments not requiring 24-hour hospitalization (chemotherapy, dialysis, etc.)
5. Ambulance Charges: Up to ₹2,000 per hospitalization
6. Organ Donor Expenses: Covered up to sum insured
7. AYUSH Treatment: Coverage for Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy treatments
8. Health Check-ups: Free annual health check-up after first claim-free year
9. Domiciliary Hospitalization: Treatment at home when hospitalization not possible
10. Maternity Coverage: Available as add-on with ₹50,000 limit

EXCLUSIONS - WHAT'S NOT COVERED
1. Cosmetic or plastic surgery (unless medically necessary)
2. Dental treatment (unless due to accident)
3. Spectacles, contact lenses, hearing aids
4. Congenital diseases and defects
5. Self-inflicted injuries and suicide attempts
6. War, nuclear risks, and terrorism-related injuries
7. Treatment outside India (unless opted for international coverage)
8. Experimental or unproven treatments
9. Obesity treatment and weight control programs
10. Infertility and assisted reproduction

SUB-LIMITS AND CO-PAYMENT
Room Rent Limit: ₹5,000 per day (1% of sum insured)
ICU Charges: ₹10,000 per day (2% of sum insured)
Co-payment: 10% of admissible claim amount for all claims
Age-based Co-pay: Additional 20% for policyholders above 60 years

CLAIM PROCESS
Claim Type: Both Cashless and Reimbursement available
Network Hospitals: 8,000+ hospitals across India
Cashless Process: Pre-authorization required, approved within 2 hours
Reimbursement: Submit documents within 15 days of discharge
Claim Settlement: 95% claims settled within 7 days

3. Reduced Waiting Period: Only 12 months for diabetes-related complications (instead of 24 months)
4. Preventive Care: Coverage for diabetes screening and HbA1c tests
5. Dietary Consultation: Free nutritionist consultation twice a year

SPECIAL FEATURES FOR HYPERTENSION PATIENTS
1. Blood Pressure Monitoring: Free BP monitor provided after first year
2. Cardiac Coverage: Full coverage for hypertension-related cardiac events after waiting period
3. Medication Support: 10% discount on prescribed hypertension medications
