Senior Secure Plan - Health Insurance for Golden Years
Insurer: LifeCare Insurance Company

POLICY OVERVIEW
Senior Secure Plan is specifically designed for individuals aged 60 and above, offering comprehensive health coverage tailored to the unique needs of senior citizens. This policy addresses age-related health concerns with minimal waiting periods and extensive coverage.

PREMIUM AND COVERAGE
Annual Premium: ₹35,000 (for individual aged 60-70)
Sum Insured: ₹3 Lakhs, ₹5 Lakhs, ₹7 Lakhs
Policy Type: Individual or Couple
Entry Age: 60 to 80 years
Renewal: Lifetime

WAITING PERIODS

No Waiting Period: Accidental injuries covered from day 1
